Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our school

How do I apply for admission?

Admission applications can be submitted online through our admissions portal. Visit the Admissions page for detailed instructions and requirements. Applications for the upcoming academic year open in May.

What are the school fees?

Our school fees vary by grade level. Please contact our finance office at finance@hss.co.za for the current fee structure. We offer sibling discounts and financial assistance programs for qualifying families.

Do you offer transportation services?

Yes, we have a dedicated bus service covering most areas in Howick and surrounding regions. Bus routes and schedules are available at the administration office. Transportation fees are separate from tuition.

How can parents get involved?

We encourage parent participation through our Parent-Teacher Association (PTA), volunteering opportunities, and school events. Contact our PTA coordinator at pta@hss.co.za to learn more about getting involved.
