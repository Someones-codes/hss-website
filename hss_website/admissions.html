Admissions

Begin your educational journey with Howick Secondary School

Admission Requirements

Essential Documentation

  • Unabridged birth certificate
  • Most recent school report
  • Immunization card
  • 2 passport photos
  • Proof of residence (utility bill)
  • Parent/guardian ID copies

Academic Requirements

Applicants must provide their final report from the previous academic year:

  • Grade 8: Grade 7 final report
  • Grade 9: Grade 8 final report
  • Grade 10: Grade 9 final report
  • Grade 11: Grade 10 final report

Key Dates

  • Grade 8 Applications: Open 1 March 2025 | Deadline: 28 February 2026
  • Grades 9-11: Rolling admissions (subject to availability)
  • Scholarship Deadline: 28 February 2026
  • Notification of Acceptance: By 31 May 2026

Fee Information

2026 Annual School Fee: R45,500

Payment plans and financial assistance available for qualifying families.

Based on South African Schools Act Section 40
