About Our School

School Overview

Howick Secondary School, located in the town of Howick in KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa, has been a pillar of educational excellence for over seven decades. Our school provides quality education to students from Grade 8 to Grade 12, preparing them for future success in both academic and personal endeavors.

With state-of-the-art facilities, dedicated educators, and a nurturing environment, HSS offers a comprehensive educational experience that balances academic rigor with character development. Our beautiful campus spans 15 hectares, featuring modern classrooms, science laboratories, a library, sports fields, and cultural facilities.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide a transformative educational experience that empowers students to achieve academic excellence, develop strong character, and become responsible global citizens who make positive contributions to society.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading educational institution in KwaZulu-Natal that nurtures innovation, celebrates diversity, and prepares students to thrive in an ever-changing world.

Our Core Values

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in all academic and extracurricular activities, encouraging students to reach their full potential.

Integrity

We uphold honesty, ethical behavior, and moral courage in all our actions and decisions.

Respect

We value diversity and treat all individuals with dignity, compassion, and understanding.

Innovation

We foster creativity and critical thinking, preparing students to solve complex problems in innovative ways.

Our History

1952

School Founded

Howick Secondary School was established with just 87 students and 5 teachers in temporary facilities.

1975

Science Wing Added

A new science wing with modern laboratories was constructed to support STEM education.

1994

Post-Apartheid Transformation

The school embraced diversity and inclusivity following South Africa's democratic transition.

2010

ICT Center Established

A state-of-the-art computer center was opened to prepare students for the digital age.

2022

70th Anniversary

The school celebrated 70 years of educational excellence with a year-long program of events.
